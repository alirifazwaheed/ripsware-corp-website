FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What industries does Ripsware serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including healthcare, finance & banking, government, telecom, retail, education, hospitality, and real estate. Our solutions are tailored to meet the specific compliance, security, and operational needs of each sector.

What types of IT support packages do you offer?

We offer flexible support packages ranging from single-outlet plans for small offices to enterprise-grade multi-outlet packages. Each plan includes different SLA tiers, response times, and coverage levels. Contact us for a customized quote.

How long does a typical software development project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple web application may take 4–8 weeks, while a full enterprise solution can take 3–6 months. We follow agile methodologies and provide detailed project timelines during the discovery phase.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes. All our projects include a post-launch support period. We also offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for continuous monitoring, updates, and technical support to keep your systems running optimally.

Can you work with our existing systems and infrastructure?

Absolutely. We specialize in integrating with existing systems including ERP platforms (SAP, Odoo), legacy applications, third-party APIs, and existing cloud infrastructure. Our goal is to enhance what you have, not replace everything.
